Description:
    Script to learn the emission probabilities out of a VCF file.

Usage:
    emission_probabilities.sh  [options]  -- VCF_FILE

Options:
    -h|--help           Help.
    -d|--outdir         Output directory. Default: $PWD.

Examples:
    emission_probabilities.sh -d out -- test.vcf

Dependancies:
    perl{Algorithm::Combinatorics}
