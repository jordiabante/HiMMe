Description:
    Script to score a FASTA file containing an assembly or simply NGS reads.

Usage:
    scoring_fasta.sh  [options]  -- TRANS_MATRIX EMISSION_PROBS FASTA_FILE

Options:
    -h|--help           Help.
    -d|--outdir         Output directory. Default: $PWD.
    -k|--kmer_size      K-mer size used. Default: 2.

Examples:
    scoring_fasta.sh -k 4 -d out -- grch37_cdna_tm4.txt.gz grch37_all_ep2991694175.txt.gz assembly.fa

Dependancies:
    perl{Algorithm::Combinatorics}
